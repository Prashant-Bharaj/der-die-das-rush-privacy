Privacy Policy for Der Die Das Rush

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Der Die Das Rush ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Der Die Das Rush (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes.
  • Game Data: We collect your game progress, scores, streaks, and statistics to provide game functionality and sync across devices.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and device identifiers for advertising and analytics purposes.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as game sessions and feature usage.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our App
  • To sync your game progress across devices when you sign in
  • To display personalized advertisements
  • To improve and optimize our App
  • To provide customer support

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Used for user account management
  • Cloud Firestore: Used for storing your game progress and statistics
  • Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Google AdMob

  • Used for displaying advertisements in the App
  • AdMob may collect device information and usage data for personalized advertising
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Guest Mode

You can use the App without creating an account (guest mode). In guest mode, your game data is stored locally on your device and is not synced to our servers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (see Account Deletion section below)
  • Opt out of data collection by using guest mode
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below or follow the account deletion process outlined in Section 8.

8. Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Der Die Das Rush account and all associated data by following these steps:

  1. Open the Der Die Das Rush app on your device
  2. Navigate to the Profile screen
  3. If you're signed in, tap on "Sign Out" to access account deletion options
  4. Alternatively, contact us directly at prashant.bharaj@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  5. Include your registered email address in the request

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your email address and authentication information
  • All game progress, scores, streaks, and statistics
  • All data stored in your account in Firebase

Data Retention Period

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. All your data will be permanently deleted within this timeframe. Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes as required by law, but this will be minimal and only where necessary.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your game progress and data will be lost.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will delete your associated data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
